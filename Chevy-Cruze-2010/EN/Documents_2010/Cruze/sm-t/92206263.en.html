Crankshaft Pulley Replacement

Special Tools

    • EN 6625 Flywheel Holder
    • EN-45059 Angle Sensor Kit

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Raise the vehicle full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  2. Remove the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement .


  3. Rotate the drive belt tensioner bolt  (2) clockwise.
  4. Remove the drive belt (1) while pulling the drive belt tensioner with a suitable tool (3).


  5. Remove the engine/transmission bolt (1).


  6. Install the EN 6625 holder  (2).
  7. Install and tighten the engine/transmission bolt (1).


  8. Remove the crankshaft pulley bolt (2).
  9. Remove the crankshaft pulley (1).

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install the crankshaft pulley (1) and NEW bolt (2) with screw locking compound and tighten in 3 passes use the EN-45059 sensor kit :
    2. 1.1. First pass to  95 N·m (70 lb ft)
    1.2. Second pass to  30°
    1.3. Third pass to  15°.


  2. Remove the engine/transmission bolt (1).
  3. Remove the EN 6625 holder  (2).


  4. Install the engine/transmission bolt and tighten to  50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  5. Rotate the drive belt tensioner bolt  (2) clockwise.
  6. Install the drive belt (1) while pulling the tensioner with a suitable tool (3).
  7. Rotate the tensioner counterclockwise to tension the belt drive belt (2)
  8. Install the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement .
  9. Lower the vehicle full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
