Front Differential Bearing Cup Installation

Front Differential Bearing Cup Installation

Callout

Component Name

1

Front Differential Bearing Cup

Procedure

  1. Tighten the front bearing differential cup until there is no end play with the differential.
  2. Tighten or loosen the front bearing differential cup to get the required preload on the bearings.

Preload Specification

    • Used Bearings: 1 N·m (9 lb in) is required to rotate the differential one revolution per second.
    • New Bearings: 2 N·m (18 lb in) is required to rotate the differential one revolution per second.
