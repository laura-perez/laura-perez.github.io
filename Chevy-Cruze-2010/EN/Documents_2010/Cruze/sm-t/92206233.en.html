Engine Mount Replacement - Right Side

Removal Procedure
  1. Open the hood.
  2. Remove the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .
  3. Remove the air cleaner assembly. Refer to Air Cleaner Assembly Replacement .
  4. Raise the vehicle by its full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  5. Install Engine/Transmission Mounting. Refer to Engine/Transmission Mounting .
  6. Lower vehicle by its full height.


  7. Unclip the cooling liquid hose from the bracket (2).
  8. Remove the engine mount nut (5).
  9. Remove the engine mount bolts (1).
  10. Remove the engine mount bolts (4).
  11. Remove the engine mount (3).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the engine mount (3).

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the engine mount bolts (4) and tighten to  40 N·m (30 lb ft).
  3. Install the engine mount bolts (1) and tighten to  60 N·m (43 lb ft).
  4. Install the engine mount nut (5) and tighten to  62 N·m (46 lb ft).
  5. Clip in the cooling liquid hose from the bracket (2).
  6. Raise the vehicle by its full height.
  7. Remove Engine/Transmission Mounting. Refer to Engine/Transmission Mounting .
  8. Lower the vehicle by its full height.
  9. Install the air cleaner assembly. Refer to Air Cleaner Assembly Replacement .
  10. Install the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .
  11. Close the hood.
