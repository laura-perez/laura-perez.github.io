Radiator Outlet Hose Replacement (1.6L LDE, LXV and 2H0)

Removal Procedure
  1. Open the hood.

    2. Warning: Unless directed otherwise, the ignition and start switch must be in the OFF or LOCK position, and all electrical loads must be OFF before servicing any electrical component. Disconnect the negative battery cable to prevent an electrical spark should a tool or equipment come in contact with an exposed electrical terminal. Failure to follow these precautions may result in personal injury and/or damage to the vehicle or its components.

  2. Disconnect the battery. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  3. Raise the vehicle by its full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  4. Remove the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement
  5. Place collecting basin underneath.
  6. Drain the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .


  7. Loosen the radiator outlet hose clamp (5).
  8. Remove the radiator outlet hose (3) from the radiator outlet pipe (4).
  9. Lower the vehicle by its full height.
  10. Loosen the radiator outlet hose clamp (2).
  11. Remove the radiator outlet hose (3) from the water pump (1).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the radiator outlet hose (3) to the water pump (1).
  2. Install the radiator outlet hose clamp (2).
  3. Raise the vehicle by its full height.
  4. Install the radiator outlet hose (3) to the radiator outlet pipe (4).
  5. Install the radiator outlet hose clamp (5).
  6. Install the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement .
  7. Lower the vehicle by its full height.
  8. Fill the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  9. Connect the battery. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
