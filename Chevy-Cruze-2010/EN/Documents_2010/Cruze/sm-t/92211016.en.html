Air Inlet Valve Actuator Replacement (RHD)

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove instrument panel compartment. Refer to Instrument Panel Compartment Replacement .


  2. Remove air inlet valve actuator gear (1).


  3. Disconnect air inlet valve actuator electrical connector (1). Remove 2 air inlet valve actuator screws (2) and remove air inlet valve actuator (3).

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install air inlet valve actuator (3) and install 2 air inlet valve actuator screws (2) and tighten to 2.5N·m(22 lb in). Install air inlet valve actuator electrical connector (1).


  2. Install air inlet valve actuator gear (1).
  3. Install instrument panel compartment. Refer to Instrument Panel Compartment Replacement .
