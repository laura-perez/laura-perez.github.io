Exhaust System Replacement (1.6L LDE, LXV, LXT and 1.8L 2H0)

Special Tools

CH 6614 Chain Pipe Cutter

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Raise the vehicle by its full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .


    2. Warning: While engine is operating, the exhaust system will become extremely hot. To prevent burns avoid contacting a hot exhaust system.

  2. Make a cut with CH 6614 cutter  (1) and separate the exhaust rear muffler (2).


    3. Note: A second person is required.

  3. Hang out the exhaust rear muffler (3) from the exhaust rear muffler insulator (1, 2).
  4. Remove the exhaust rear muffler.


    5. Note: A second person is required.

  5. Remove the 4 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket bolts (4, 5).
  6. Remove the 2 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket insulator nuts (3).
  7. Remove the exhaust pipe front hanger bracket (2).
  8. Remove the 2 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket insulators (1).


    9. Note: A second person is required.

  9. Remove the heated oxygen sensor (1).
  10. Remove the 3 catalytic converter to exhaust pipe (2) nuts (3).
  11. Remove the catalytic converter to exhaust to exhaust pipe gasket (4).
  12. Remove the exhaust system.

Installation Procedure

    Warning: While engine is operating, the exhaust system will become extremely hot. To prevent burns avoid contacting a hot exhaust system.

    Note: A second person is required.

  1. Install the exhaust system.


  2. Install the NEW catalytic converter to exhaust pipe gasket (4).

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the 3 NEW catalytic converter to exhaust pipe (2) nuts (3) and tighten to  20 N·m (15 lb ft).
  4. Install the heated oxygen sensor (1) and tighten to 40 N·m (30 lb ft).


  5. Install the 2 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket insulators (1).
  6. Install the exhaust pipe front hanger bracket (2).
  7. Install the 2 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket insulator nuts (3) and tighten to  20 N·m (15 lb ft).
  8. Install the 4 exhaust pipe front hanger bracket bolts (4, 5) and tighten to  25 N·m (19 lb ft).


    9. Note: A second person is required.

  9. Install the exhaust rear muffler.
  10. Hang in the exhaust rear muffler (3) at position (2, 4).

    11. Note: Use a NEW exhaust pipe clamp.

  11. Connect the exhaust rear muffler with the muffler through an exhaust pipe clamp at position (1) and tighten the clamp to  20 N·m (15 lb ft).
  12. Lower the vehicle by its full height.
