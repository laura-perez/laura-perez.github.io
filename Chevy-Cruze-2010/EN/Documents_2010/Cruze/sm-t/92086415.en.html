Caution: Compress the reluctor wheel just enough to clear the retainer. Over compressing the reluctor wheel will break the alignment tab and the clutch housing.

Caution: Regulate the air pressure to 40 psi maximum. High pressure could cause the piston to over travel and damage the piston seals.

Tip
Place the housing assembly onto the input shaft support inside the case. Apply shop air to the clutch fluid feed hole in the case to verify proper piston operation.
