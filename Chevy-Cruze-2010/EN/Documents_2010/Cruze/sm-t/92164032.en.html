Air Cleaner Assembly Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Open the hood.


  2. Disconnect the intake air temperature sensor wiring harness plug (1).
  3. Remove the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement .


  4. Remove the air cleaner assembly (1).
    5. • Unclip the air cleaner inlet duct
    • Unclip the fluid reservoir hose

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the air cleaner assembly (1).
    2. • Clip in the fluid reservoir hose
    • Clip in the air cleaner inlet duct
  2. Install the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement .


  3. Connect the intake air temperature sensor wiring harness plug (1).
  4. Close the hood.
