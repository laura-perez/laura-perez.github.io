Ignition Coil Removal

Special Tools

EN 49424 Remover and Installer Ignition Module

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

    Note: Note the arrow on the cover.

  1. Remove the cover of the DIS ignition coil in the direction of the arrow.
  2. Remove the 2 ignition coil bolts.


  3. Install the EN 49424 remover/installer  (1).


  4. Remove the ignition coil module (2) with the EN 49424 remover/installer  (1).
