Radiator Replacement (2.0L Diesel LLW)

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the front bumper fascia. Refer to Front Bumper Fascia Replacement .
  2. Remove the radiator grille reinforcement support. Refer to Radiator Grille Reinforcement Support Replacement .
  3. Remove the hood primary and secondary latch. Refer to Hood Primary and Secondary Latch Replacement .
  4. Remove the intake air duct. Refer to Intake Air Duct Replacement .
  5. Drain the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  6. Remove the cooling lines. Refer to Radiator Inlet Hose Replacement and Radiator Outlet Hose Replacement .
  7. Remove the radiator support brackets. Refer to Radiator Support Bracket Replacement .
  8. Remove the charge air cooler. Refer to Charge Air Cooler Replacement .
  9. Remove the engine coolant fan. Refer to Engine Coolant Fan Replacement .
  10. Remove the air conditioning condensor. Refer to Air Conditioning Condenser Replacement .


  11. Remove the radiator air seal (1).


  12. Remove the radiator (1).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the radiator (1).


  2. Install the radiator air seal (1)
  3. Install the air conditioning condensor. Refer to Air Conditioning Condenser Replacement .
  4. Install the engine coolant fan. Refer to Engine Coolant Fan Replacement .
  5. Install the charge air cooler. Refer to Charge Air Cooler Replacement .
  6. Install the radiator support brackets. Refer to Radiator Support Bracket Replacement .
  7. Install the cooling lines. Refer to Radiator Inlet Hose Replacement and Radiator Outlet Hose Replacement .
  8. Fill the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  9. Install the intake air duct. Refer to Intake Air Duct Replacement .
  10. Install the hood primary and secondary latch. Refer to Hood Primary and Secondary Latch Replacement .
  11. Install the radiator grille reinforcement support. Refer to Radiator Grille Reinforcement Support Replacement .
  12. Install the front bumper fascia. Refer to Front Bumper Fascia Replacement .
