Battery Positive and Negative Cable Replacement (1.6L LDE, LXV and 1.8L 2H0)

  1. Disconnect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  2. Remove the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement .


  3. Unlock the retaining tabs on the battery fuse box cover, and open the cover (1).
  4. Remove the positive battery cable to starter nut (2).
  5. Remove the positive battery cable to starter (3) from the battery.


  6. Remove the negative cable nut (1).
  7. Remove the negative cable (2).


  8. Remove the negative cable nut (2) from the starter motor (7).
  9. Remove the negative cable (1).
  10. Remove the 2 positive cable nuts (4, 5).
  11. Remove the 2 positive cables (3, 6).


  12. Remove the rubber cap from the positive cable generator connector.
  13. Remove the positive cable nut (1) from the generator (3).
  14. Remove the positive cable (2) from the generator (3).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the positive cable (2) to the generator (3).

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the positive cable nut (1) to the generator (3) and tighten to  7 N·m (62 lb in).
  3. Install the rubber cap to the positive cable generator connector.


  4. Install the 2 positive cables (3, 6).
  5. Install the 2 positive cable nuts (4, 5) and tighten to  12.5 N·m (111 lb in).
  6. Install the negative cable (1).
  7. Install the negative cable nut (2) to the starter motor (7) and tighten to  12.5 N·m (110 lb in).


  8. Install the negative cable (2).
  9. Install the negative cable nut (1) and tighten to  9 N·m (80 lb in).


  10. Install the positive battery cable to starter (3) at the battery.
  11. Install the positive battery cable to starter nut (2) and tighten to  9 N·m (80 lb in).
  12. Close the cover (1) and lock the retaining tabs on the battery fuse box cover.
  13. Install the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement .
  14. Connect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
