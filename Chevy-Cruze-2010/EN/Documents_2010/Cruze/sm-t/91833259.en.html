Fuel in Engine Oil

Definition : If fuel is suspected of leaking into the crank case the following procedure should be performed to verify the condition.

  1. Remove the oil level indicator and allow oil to drip onto a clean white paper towel.
  2. If the oil is diluted with fuel, it will become apparent as the towel wicks the fuel away from the drip of oil on the towel. The fuel will expand out in a ring around the oil droplet.
  3. If the fuel dilution is apparent, inspect the vehicle for aftermarket performance accessories that may cause damage to the injection pump. After repairs are completed, perform the test again to verify the condition is corrected.
  4. If no fuel dilution is present, verify the oil level and correct as needed.
