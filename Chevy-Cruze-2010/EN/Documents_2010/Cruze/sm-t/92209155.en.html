Camshaft Gear Replacement

Special Tools

    • EN 6347 Brace Holder
    •  EN 956-1 Brace

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Open hood.
  2. Remove the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement .


    3. Note: Two mechanics are required.

  3. Install the EN 6347 brace  (1) and the EN956-1 brace holder  (2) in the exhaust camshaft sprocket.
  4. Loosen the locking bolts (3) from EN 6347 brace .

    5. Note: The grooves of EN 6347 brace must locate in the camshaft sprocket. Check for correct seating.

  5. Tighten the 2 locking bolts (3).
  6. Loosen the exhaust camshaft sprocket bolt (4) while holding the exhaust camshaft sprocket with EN 6347 brace .
  7. Remove the exhaust camshaft sprocket bolt (4).
  8. Remove the exhaust camshaft sprocket.
  9. Repeat the same procedure at the intake camshaft sprocket for removal.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the camshaft sprockets (3).

    2. Note: Turn the sprockets around till them locked in correct position.

  2. Install the camshaft sprocket washers (2) and the camshaft sprocket bolts (1) and hand tighten.


  3. Install EN 6347 brace and EN 956-1 holder .

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Tighten the bolts to  67.5 N·m (49 lb ft).
  5. Install the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement .
  6. Close the hood.
