Oil Pan Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Open Hood.
  2. Raise the vehicle full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  3. Remove the exhaust flexible pipe. Refer to Exhaust Flexible Pipe Replacement .
  4. Drain the engine oil. Refer to Engine Oil and Oil Filter Replacement .


  5. Remove the 3 oil pan to transmission bolts (2).
  6. Remove the 15 oil pan bolts (3).

    7. Note: 

       • Use a suitable tool.
       • Be carefully do not damage the oil pan and oil pan sealing surface. Refer to Separating Parts .

  7. Remove the oil pan (1).

Installation Procedure


    Note: 

       • Ensure that the oil pan sealing surface (2) is clear of oil, grease and old gasket material.
       • Do not coat sealant on the oil pan bolt holes, it could damage the oil pan bolt threads or the engine block.

  1. Inspect the oil pan (1) of damages, nicks and cracks. Refer to Oil Pan Cleaning and Inspection .
  2. Coat sealant on the oil pan sealing surface (2).


  3. Install the oil pan (1).

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Install the 15 oil pan bolts (3) and tighten to  10 N·m (89 lb in).
  5. Install the 3 oil pan to transmission bolts (2) and tighten to  35 N·m (26 lb ft).
  6. Install the exhaust flexible pipe. Refer to Exhaust Flexible Pipe Replacement
  7. Lower the vehicle full height.
  8. Fill in NEW engine oil. Refer to Engine Oil and Oil Filter Replacement .
  9. Close the hood.
