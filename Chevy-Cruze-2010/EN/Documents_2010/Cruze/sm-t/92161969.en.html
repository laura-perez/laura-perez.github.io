Circuit/System Description

The instrument cluster turns ON the ABS indicator for 5 s after ignition ON or when the electronic brake control module (EBCM) detects a fault and sends a serial data message to the instrument cluster commanding the indicator ON.

Circuit/System Verification

Ignition ON, the ABS indicator should turn ON. After 5 s the ABS indicator should turn OFF.

Circuit/System Testing

  1. Ignition ON, with a scan tool command the instrument cluster All Indicators test ON and OFF. Verify the ABS indicator turns ON and OFF.
    2. ⇒If the ABS indicator does not turn ON and OFF, replace the P16 Instrument Cluster.
  2. Verify the ABS indicator turns OFF 5 s after ignition ON.
    3. ⇒If the ABS indicator does not turn OFF, replace the K17 Electronic Brake Control Module (EBCM).
