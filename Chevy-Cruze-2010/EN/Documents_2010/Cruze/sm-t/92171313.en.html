Fuel Injection Pump Replacement

Special Tools

EN-6015 Fuel Line Plug Kit

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement .
  2. Remove the fuel pump drive pulley. Refer to Fuel Injection Pump Drive Pulley Removal .
  3. Remove the fuel feed hose. Refer to Fuel Hose Replacement - Fuel Injection Pump to Fuel Feed Block .


  4. Remove the hose clamp (1).
  5. Remove the hose (3) from the fuel injection pump.
  6. Install the EN-6015 plug to seal the hose and the fuel injection pump.
  7. Remove the intake manifold. Refer to Intake Manifold Replacement .


  8. Remove the 3 fuel pump bolts (2).
  9. Remove the fuel pump (1).

Installation Procedure


    Note: Do not blow air with an air gun onto injection pump. If contaminated, wipe clean with an oil paper.

  1. Install the fuel pump (1).

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the 3 fuel pump bolts (2) and tighten to  30 N·m (23 lb ft).
  3. Install the intake manifold. Refer to Intake Manifold Replacement .
  4. Remove the EN-6015 plug .


  5. Install the hose to the fuel injection pump (3).
  6. Install the hose clamp (1).
  7. Install the fuel feed hose. Refer to Fuel Hose Replacement - Fuel Injection Pump to Fuel Feed Block .
  8. Install the fuel pump drive pulley. Refer to Fuel Injection Pump Drive Pulley Installation .
  9. Install the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement .
