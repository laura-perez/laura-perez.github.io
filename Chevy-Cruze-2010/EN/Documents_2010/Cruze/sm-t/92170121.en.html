Fuel Sender Assembly Replacement

Removal Procedure

Warning: Refer to Gasoline/Gasoline Vapors Warning in the Preface section.

  1. Remove and disassemble fuel tank. Refer to Fuel Tank Replacement .


  2. Disconnect the fuel sender assembly wiring harness plug (1).
  3. Unclip the 2 fuel sender retainer pins.
  4. Remove the fuel sender assembly (2).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the fuel sender assembly (2).
  2. Connect the fuel sender assembly wiring harness plug (1).
  3. Assemble and install the fuel tank. Refer to Fuel Tank Replacement .
