Exhaust Manifold Replacement (1.6L LXT)

Removal Procedure

Warning: Refer to Hot Exhaust System Warning in the Preface section.

Warning: Refer to Protective Goggles and Glove Warning in the Preface section.

  1. Open hood.
  2. Remove the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .


  3. Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor wiring harness plug (1).
  4. Remove the exhaust manifold heat shield. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Heat Shield Replacement .


  5. Remove the 3 catalytic converter nuts (1).
  6. Raise the vehicle by its full height. Refer to  Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  7. Remove the exhaust flexible pipe. Refer to Exhaust Flexible Pipe Replacement .


  8. Remove the 3 catalytic converter lower nuts (5).
  9. Remove the catalytic converter bracket bolt (3).
  10. Remove the catalytic converter bracket (4).


  11. Remove the catalytic converter (4).
  12. Remove the catalytic converter upper gasket.
  13. Lower the vehicle full height.


  14. Loosen the 10 exhaust manifold nuts in a sequence as in the graphic shown.


  15. Remove the engine lift bracket nut  (4).
  16. Remove the engine lift bracket (1).
  17. Remove the exhaust manifold (2).
  18. Remove the 9 exhaust manifold nuts.
  19. Remove the exhaust manifold gasket (3).

Installation Procedure


    Note: Ensure that the exhaust manifold and cylinder head surface is clear of old gasket material.

  1. Install a NEW exhaust manifold gasket (3).
  2. Install the exhaust manifold (2).
  3. Install the engine lift bracket (1).

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Install a NEW engine lift bracket nut  (4) and tighten to  25N·m (18 lb ft)in the sequence as shown in the graphic below.


  5. Install 9 NEW exhaust manifold nuts in the sequence as shown in the graphic and tighten to  22N·m (17 lb ft)..
  6. Raise the vehicle full height.


  7. Install a NEW catalytic converter upper gasket.
  8. Install the catalytic converter (4).


  9. Install the catalytic converter bracket (4).
  10. Install the 3 catalytic converter lower nuts (5) and tighten to  50 N·m (37 lb ft)..
  11. Install the catalytic converter bracket bolt (3) and tighten to  50 N·m (37 lb ft)..
  12. Install the exhaust flexible pipe. Refer to Exhaust Flexible Pipe Replacement .
  13. Lower the vehicle full height.


  14. Install 3 NEW catalytic converter nuts (1) and tighten to and tighten to  40 N·m(30 lb ft).
  15. Install the exhaust manifold heat shield. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Heat Shield Replacement .


  16. Connect the heated oxygen sensor wiring harness plug (1).
  17. Install the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .
  18. Close the hood.
