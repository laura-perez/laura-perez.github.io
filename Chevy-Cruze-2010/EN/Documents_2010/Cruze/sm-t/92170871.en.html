Timing Belt Rear Cover Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the camshaft position actuator adjuster. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Adjuster Replacement .
  2. Remove the timing belt lower front cover. Refer to Timing Belt Lower Front Cover Replacement .
  3. Remove the timing belt center front cover. Refer to Timing Belt Center Front Cover Replacement .
  4. Remove the timing belt tensioner. Refer to Timing Belt Tensioner Replacement .


  5. Remove the 4 timing belt rear cover bolts (2).

    6. Note: Oil can escape. Use a cloth, rag or paper to take the oil drain away from all timing components.

  6. Remove the timing belt rear cover (1).

Installation Procedure
  1. Clean the 4 timing belt rear cover threads.
  2. Apply locking compound to the 4 NEW timing belt rear cover bolts.


    3. Note: If the cover is contaminated with oil. You have to clean it close.

  3. Install the timing belt rear cover (1).

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Install the 4  timing belt rear cover bolts (2) and tighten to  6 N·m (53 lb in).
  5. Install the timing belt tensioner. Refer to Timing Belt Tensioner Replacement .
  6. Install the timing belt center front cover. Refer to Timing Belt Center Front Cover Replacement .
  7. Install the timing belt lower front cover. Refer to Timing Belt Lower Front Cover Replacement .
  8. Install the camshaft position actuator adjuster. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Adjuster Replacement .
