Cylinder Head Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the intake manifold. Refer to Intake Manifold Replacement .
  2. Remove the exhaust manifold. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Replacement .
  3. Remove the timing belt tensioner. Refer to Timing Belt Tensioner Replacement .


  4. Remove the 2 engine oil cooler pipe bolts (1).
  5. Remove the engine oil cooler pipe (2).
  6. Disconnect the 3 coolant hoses from coolant flange.
  7. Remove the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement .
  8. Remove the cylinder head. Refer to Cylinder Head Removal .
  9. Remove the 2 camshaft position sensor. Refer to Camshaft Position Sensor Replacement .
  10. Remove the 2 camshaft position actuator solenoid valve. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Solenoid Valve Replacement .
  11. Remove the 2 camshaft position actuator adjuster. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Adjuster Removal .
  12. Remove the timing belt rear cover. Refer to Timing Belt Rear Cover Removal .
  13. Remove thermostat housing. Refer to Engine Coolant Thermostat Housing Removal .

Installation Procedure
  1. Install thermostat housing. Refer to Engine Coolant Thermostat Housing Installation .
  2. Install the timing belt rear cover. Refer to Timing Belt Rear Cover Installation .
  3. Install the camshaft position actuator adjuster. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Adjuster Installation .
  4. Install the camshaft position actuator solenoid valve. Refer to Camshaft Position Actuator Solenoid Valve Replacement .
  5. Install the 2 camshaft position sensor. Refer to Camshaft Position Sensor Replacement .
  6. Install the cylinder head. Refer to Cylinder Head Installation .
  7. Install the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement .
  8. Connect the 3 coolant hoses from coolant flange.


  9. Install the engine oil cooler pipe (2).

    10. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  10. Install the 2 engine oil cooler pipe bolts (1) and tighten to  9 N·m (80 lb in).
  11. Install the timing belt tensioner. Refer to Timing Belt Tensioner Replacement .
  12. Install the exhaust manifold. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Replacement .
  13. Install the intake manifold. Refer to Intake Manifold Replacement .
  14. Fill the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  15. Check and correct the engine oil.
