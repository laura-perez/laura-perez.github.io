Cylinder Head Replacement

Special Tools

EN-45059 Torque Angle Sensor Kit .

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Open the hood.
  2. Remove the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement .
  3. Remove the exhaust manifold. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Replacement .
  4. Remove the timing belt rear cover. Refer to Timing Belt Rear Cover Replacement .
  5. Remove the engine coolant thermostat. Refer to Engine Coolant Thermostat Replacement .
  6. Remove the generator adjust bracket. Refer to Generator Adjust Bracket Removal .
  7. Remove the intake manifold brace. Refer to Intake Manifold Brace Removal .


  8. Remove the 10 cylinder head bolts in sequence as shown.
    9. 8.1. Loosen the 10 bolts  90°.
    8.2. Loosen the 10 bolts  180°.

    Note: Two Mechanics required.

  9. Remove the cylinder head and place on a suitable base.
  10. Remove the cylinder head gasket.
  11. Remove the intake manifold from the cylinder head. Refer to Intake Manifold Removal .
  12. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation adapter. Refer to Exhaust Gas Recirculation Valve Adapter Replacement .
  13. Remove the ignition coil. Refer to Ignition Coil Removal .


  14. Remove the 3 ignition coil bracket nuts (2).
  15. Remove the ignition coil bracket (1).
  16. For cylinder head disassemble. Refer to Cylinder Head Disassemble .

Installation Procedure
  1. For cylinder head assemble. Refer to Cylinder Head Assemble .


  2. Install the ignition coil bracket (1).
  3. Install the 3 ignition coil bracket nuts (2).
  4. Remove the ignition coil. Refer to Ignition Coil Installation .
  5. Install the exhaust gas recirculation adapter. Refer to Exhaust Gas Recirculation Valve Adapter Replacement .
  6. Remove the intake manifold to the cylinder head. Refer to Intake Manifold Installation .
  7. Clean the sealing surfaces.
  8. Inspect for plane surface.
    9. • Cylinder block, cylinder head
    • Use a straight-edge
  9. Install the cylinder head gasket.

    10. Note: Two Mechanics required.

  10. Install the cylinder head.

    11. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



    Note: Note the correct tightening sequence.

  11. Install NEW cylinder head bolts.
  12. Tighten the bolts in 5 passes. Use the EN-45059 sensor kit .
    13. • First pass to  25 N·m (18 lb ft)
    • Second pass to  60°
    • Third pass to  60°
    • Fourth pass to  60°
    • Fifth pass to  10°
  13. Install the intake manifold brace. Refer to Intake Manifold Brace Installation .
  14. Install the generator adjust bracket. Refer to Generator Adjust Bracket Installation .
  15. Install the engine coolant thermostat. Refer to Engine Coolant Thermostat Replacement .
  16. Install the timing belt rear cover. Refer to Timing Belt Rear Cover Replacement .
  17. Install the exhaust manifold. Refer to Exhaust Manifold Replacement .
  18. Install the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement .
  19. Close the hood.
