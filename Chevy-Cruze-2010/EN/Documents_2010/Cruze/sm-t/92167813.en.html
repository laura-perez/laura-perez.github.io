Manual Transmission Shift Lever and Selector Lever Cable Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
  2. Remove the front floor console. Refer to Front Floor Console Replacement .


  3. Remove the shift lever and selector lever cable adjust locks (1).


  4. Remove the shift lever and selector lever cable retainer (1) from the transmission control.
  5. Disconnect the shift lever and selector lever cables (2) from the shift control adjusters.


  6. Disconnect the shift lever and selector lever cables from the shift control lever (2) and the selector and shift lever cable bracket (1).


  7. Remove the shift lever and selector lever cable grommet (1) from the bulkhead.
  8. Remove the shift lever and selector lever cables.

Installation Procedure
  1. Install the shift lever and selector lever cables.


  2. Install the shift lever and selector lever cable grommet (1) to the bulkhead.


  3. Connect the shift lever and selector lever cables to the shift control lever (2) and to the selector and shift lever cable bracket (1).


  4. Connect the shift lever and selector lever cables (2) to the shift control adjusters.
  5. Install the shift lever and selector lever cable retainer (1) to the transmission control.
  6. Adjust the shift lever and selector lever cables. Refer to Manual Transmission Shift Lever and Selector Lever Cable Replacement .
  7. Install the front floor console. Refer to Front Floor Console Replacement .
  8. Install the battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
