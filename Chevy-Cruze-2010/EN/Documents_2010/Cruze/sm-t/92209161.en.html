Crankshaft Front Oil Seal Replacement

Special Tools

    • EN 6347 Brace Holder
    •  EN 956-1 Brace

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Open hood.
  2. Remove the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement .


  3. Remove the timing belt idler pulley bolt (2).
  4. Remove the timing belt idler pulley (1).


  5. Remove the 4 engine mount bracket bolts (2, 3).
  6. Remove the engine mount bracket (1).


  7. Remove the 3 timing belt tensioner bolts (2).
  8. Remove the timing belt tensioner (1).


    9. Note: Two mechanics are required.

  9. Install the EN 6347 brace  (1) and the EN 956-1 holder  (2) in the exhaust camshaft sprocket.
  10. Loosen the locking bolts (3) from EN 6347 brace .

    11. Note: The grooves of EN 6347 brace must locate in the camshaft sprocket. Check for correct seating.

  11. Tighten the 2 locking bolts (3).
  12. Loosen the exhaust camshaft sprocket bolt (4) while holding the exhaust camshaft sprocket with EN 6347 brace .
  13. Remove the exhaust camshaft sprocket bolt (4).
  14. Remove the exhaust camshaft sprocket.
  15. Repeat the same procedure at the intake camshaft sprocket for removal.


  16. Remove the 4 timing belt rear cover bolts (1, 3).
  17. Remove the timing belt rear cover (2).
  18. Remove the crankshaft front oil seal. Refer to Crankshaft Front Oil Seal Removal .

Installation Procedure
  1. Install the crankshaft front oil seal. Refer to Crankshaft Front Oil Seal Installation .


  2. Install the timing belt rear cover (2).
  3. Install the 4 timing belt rear cover bolts (1, 3) and tighten to  10 N·m (89 lb in).


  4. Install the camshaft sprockets (3).

    5. Note: Turn the sprockets around till them locked in correct position.

  5. Install the camshaft sprocket washers (2) and the camshaft sprocket bolts (1) and hand tighten.


  6. Install EN 6347 brace and EN 956-1 holder .

    7. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  7. Tighten the bolts to  67.5 N·m (49 lb ft).


  8. Clean the timing belt tensioner threads.
  9. Install the timing belt tensioner (1).


  10. Install the engine mount bracket (1).
  11. Install the 4 engine mount bracket bolts (2, 3) and tighten to  65 N·m (48 lb ft).
  12. Install the 3 timing belt tensioner bolts (2) and tighten to  25 N·m (18 lb ft).


  13. Clean the timing belt idler pulley bolt thread inside the cylinder head.
  14. Install the timing belt idler pulley (1).
  15. Install the timing belt idler pulley bolt (2) and tighten to  40 N·m (30 lb ft).
  16. Install the timing belt. Refer to (Timing Belt Replacement) Timing Belt Replacement .
  17. Close the hood.
