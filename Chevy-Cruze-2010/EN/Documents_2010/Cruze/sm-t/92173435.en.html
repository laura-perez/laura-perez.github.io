Front Wheel Bearing and Hub Replacement

Special Tools

EN-45059 Torque Angle Sensor Kit

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Raise and support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  2. Remove the brake rotor. Refer to Front Brake Rotor Replacement .


  3. Remove the wheel speed sensor screw (2).
  4. Remove the wheel speed sensor (1) from the steering knuckle.
  5. Remove the wheel drive shaft from the front wheel bearing/hub. Refer to front wheel drive shaft replacement Front Wheel Drive Shaft Replacement - Left Side and/or Front Wheel Drive Shaft Replacement - Right Side .


  6. Remove and discard the front wheel bearing/hub bolts (1).


  7. Remove the front wheel bearing/hub (2) and front brake shield (2) from the steering knuckle.

Installation Procedure


  1. Position the front brake shield (1) and front wheel bearing/hub (2) assembly in the steering knuckle.

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  2. Install the NEW front wheel bearing/hub bolts (1).
  3. Tighten the bearing/hub bolts (1) in 3 passes. Use the EN-45059 kit .
    4. • First pass to 90 N·m (67 lb ft)
    • Second pass to 60°
    • Third pass to 15°
  4. Install the wheel drive shaft at the front wheel bearing/hub. Refer to Front Wheel Drive Shaft Replacement - Left Side or Front Wheel Drive Shaft Replacement - Right Side .


  5. Install the wheel speed senor (1) to the steering knuckle.
  6. Install the wheel speed sensor screw (2) and tighten to  6 N·m (54 lb in).
  7. Install the brake rotor. Refer to Front Brake Rotor Replacement .
  8. Remove the support and lower the vehicle.
