Prepare the CH-49290 for Mounting Engine


  1. Install the tool components at the CH-49290-1 plate  (2).
    2. • CH-49290-16 supporting with
      CH-49290-15 plate  (3) at position 21 and V.
    • CH-49290-5 bracket  (6) at position 02.
    • CH-49290-6 pin  (5) at position E on the
       CH-49290-5 bracket  (6).
    • CH-49290-14 supporting with
      CH-49290-12 plate  (7) at position 13 and Q.
    • 2 bolts with 2  CH49290-18 supporting  (10) at position 32 and position 40.
    • CH-49290-4 bracket  (13) at position 01.
    • CH-49290-6 pin  (14) at position B on the CH-49290-4 bracket  (13).
  2. Turn the CH49290-18 supporting  (10), CH-49290-14 supporting  (7) and CH-49290-16 supporting  (3) downward.

Note: Do not Install the CH-49290-10 bracket  (1), CH-49290-11 bracket  (4), CH-49290-7 plate  (8), CH-49290-9 bracket  (9), CH-49290-7 plate  (12) and CH-49290-8 bracket  (11) until the tool contacts the frame.

Installation Procedure
  1. Raise the vehicle by its full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  2. Remove the front compartment insulator. Refer to Front Compartment Insulator Replacement .
  3. Install the CH-49290 mounting at the CH-904 frame .


    4. Note: Ensure that the 2  CH-49290-6 pins are fixed in both holes of the sub frame.

  4. Raise the CH-49290 mounting  (1) with a jack.
  5. Install the CH-49290-9 bracket and CH-49290-8 bracket  (2) with the 2 CH-49290-7 plate  (4).
  6. Install the 4 bolts (3).
  7. Install the CH-49290-10 bracket and CH-49290-11 bracket  (5).
  8. Install the 4 bolts (6).
  9. Lower the jack and remove it.


  10. Turn the CH-49290-14 supporting  (4) upwards until it seats solidly at the front engine mount bracket (3).
  11. Turn the CH-49290-16 supporting  (5) upwards until it seats solidly at the rear engine mount bracket (2).
  12. Turn the 2 CH49290-18 supporting  (6) upwards until it seats solidly at the oil pan (1).
  13. Remove the CH-904 frame from the CH-49290 mounting .

Removal Procedure
  1. Install the CH-904 frame to the CH-49290 mounting .


  2. Support the CH-49290 mounting  (1) with a jack.
  3. Remove the 4 bolts (3).
  4. Remove the CH-49290-9 bracket and CH-49290-8 bracket  (2) with the 2 CH-49290-7 plate  (4).
  5. Remove the 4 bolts (6).
  6. Remove the CH-49290-10 bracket and CH-49290-11 bracket  (5).
  7. Lower the CH-49290 mounting with a jack.
