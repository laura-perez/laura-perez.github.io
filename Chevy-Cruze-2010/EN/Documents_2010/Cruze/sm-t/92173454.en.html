Oil Pump Cleaning and Inspection

Cleaning Procedure
  1. Clean the oil pump components with non-corrosive solvent.

    2. Warning: Refer to Safety Glasses and Compressed Air Warning in the Preface section.

  2. Dry the oil pump components with compressed air.

Visual Inspection


  1. Inspect the oil pump housing for the following:
    2. • Damage, scoring, or debris on the housing surface for the drive gear (1)
    • Damage, scoring, or debris in the oil pump relief valve bore (2)
    • Damage to the oil pump housing mounting points (3)
    • Damage to the threads in the oil pump housing for the oil pump cover bolts (4)
    • Damage, scoring, or debris on the housing surface (5)
    • Damage, scoring, or debris in the oil pump output port  (6)


  2. Inspect the oil pump for the following conditions:
    3. • Damage to the oil pump cover mounting points (1)
    • Damage to the oil pump oil pump drive shaft gear (2)
    • Damage to the sealing surface between the oil pump and the oil pump housing (3)
    • Damage to the oil pump outer rotor (4)
    • Damage to the oil pump inner rotor (5)
