Circuit/System Description

The instrument cluster displays the engine coolant temperature, vehicle speed and the engine speed based on the information from the engine control module (ECM). The ECM sends appropriate information via a High Speed CAN-Bus signal to the body control module (BCM). The BCM then sends the information via a Low Speed CAN-Bus signal to the instrument cluster to display the engine coolant temperature, the engine speed, the vehicle speed and the distance travelled, either in kilometers or miles, based on the vehicle requirements.

Circuit/System Verification

with trip odometer

  1. Ignition ON, perform the Instrument Cluster Gauge Sweep test with the scan tool, verify that all gages sweep from their low to high position.
    2. ⇒If any gage does not sweep, replace the P16 Instrument Cluster.
  2. Drive the vehicle, verify the odometer and the trip odometer display, the displayed distance counts up while driving.
    3. ⇒If any odometer display does not count up, replace the P16 Instrument Cluster.
  3. If all gages do sweep and the odometer displays counts up, replace the K20 Engine Control Module.

without trip odometer

  1. Ignition ON, perform the Instrument Cluster Gauge Sweep test with the scan tool, verify that all gages sweep from their low to high position.
    2. ⇒If any gage does not sweep, replace the P16 Instrument Cluster.
  2. Drive the vehicle, verify the odometer display, the displayed distance counts up while driving.
    3. ⇒If the odometer display does not count up, replace the P16 Instrument Cluster.
  3. If all gages do sweep and the odometer display counts up, replace the K20 Engine Control Module.
