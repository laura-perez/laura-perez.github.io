Vent Valve Lever Replacement (LHD)

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove instrument panel compartment assembly. Refer to Instrument Panel Compartment Replacement .


  2. Disconnect electrical connector and remove 3 mode control cam actuator screws (2) and remove mode control cam actuator (1).


    3. Note: The graphic shows air inlet housing without attaching parts for better clarity.

  3. Remove air inlet valve and inside air valve control cam (1).


  4. Remove 2 vent valve levers (1).

Installation Procedure


    Note: The vent valve levers have to be fitted to the according vent valve gears in figured position. The air circulation dampers have to be in middle position.

  1. Install 2 vent valve levers (1).


    2. Note: The air inlet valve and inside air valve control cam can only be fitted in one position.

  2. Install air inlet valve and inside air valve control cam (1).

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  3. Install mode control cam actuator (1) and Install 3 mode control cam actuator screws (2) and connect electrical connector.

    4. Tighten
    2.5 N·m (23 lb in)

  4. Install instrument panel compartment assembly. Refer to Instrument Panel Compartment Replacement .
