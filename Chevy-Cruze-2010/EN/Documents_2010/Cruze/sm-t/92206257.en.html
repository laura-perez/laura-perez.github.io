Timing Belt Lower Front Cover Replacement

Special Tools

    • EN 6625 Flywheel Holder
    • EN 45059 Angle Sensor Kit

Removal Procedure
  1. Raise the vehicle full height. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  2. Remove the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement .


  3. Rotate the drive belt tensioner bolt  (2) to clockwise.
  4. Remove the drive belt (1) while pulling the drive belt tensioner by a suitable tool (3).


  5. Remove the engine/transmission bolt.


  6. Install the EN 6625 holder  (2).
  7. Install and tighten the engine/transmission bolt (1).


  8. Remove the crankshaft pulley bolt (2).
  9. Remove the crankshaft pulley (1).


  10. Remove the 3 timing belt lower front cover bolts (1).
  11. Remove the timing belt lower front cover (2).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the timing belt lower front cover (2).

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the 3 timing belt lower front cover bolts (1) and tighten to  10 N·m (89 lb in).

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  3. Install the crankshaft pulley (1) and NEW bolt (2) with screw locking compound and tighten in 3 passes using the EN-45059 sensor kit :
    4. 3.1. First pass to  95 N·m (70 lb ft)
    3.2. Second pass to  30°
    3.3. Third pass to  15°


  4. Remove the engine/transmission bolt (1).
  5. Remove the EN 6625 holder  (2).


  6. Install the engine/transmission bolt and tighten to  50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  7. Rotate the drive belt tensioner bolt  (2) to clockwise.
  8. Install the drive belt (1) while pulling the tensioner by a suitable tool (3).
  9. Rotate the tensioner counterclockwise to tension the belt drive belt (2).
  10. Install the front compartment splash shield. Refer to Front Compartment Splash Shield Replacement .
  11. Lower the vehicle full height.
  12. Close the hood.
