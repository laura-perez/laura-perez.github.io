Repairing Damaged Wire Insulation

If the conductive portion of the wire is not damaged, locate the problem and apply tape around the wire. If the damage is more extensive, replace the faulty segment of the wire. Refer to Splicing Copper Wire Using Splice Sleeves and follow the instruction to repair the wire.
