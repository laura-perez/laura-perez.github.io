Fuel Injector and Fuel Rail Replacement

Special Tools

CH-807 Closure Plugs

Removal Procedure

Warning: Refer to Gasoline/Gasoline Vapors Warning in the Preface section.

Warning: Refer to Actions to Take When Working with Fuel Warning in the Preface section.

Warning: Refer to Safety Goggles and Fuel Warning in the Preface section.

  1. Open the hood.
  2. Remove the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .
  3. Disconnect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  4. Disconnect the wiring harness from the fuel injectors.
  5. Place a collecting basin underneath.
  6. Disconnect the quick-release fitting of the fuel feed pipe. Refer to Plastic Collar Quick Connect Fitting Service .
  7. Close the fuel feed pipe and the fuel rail with CH-807 plug.

    8. Warning: Gasoline or gasoline vapors are highly flammable. A fire could occur if an ignition source is present. Never drain or store gasoline or diesel fuel in an open container, due to the possibility of fire or explosion. Have a dry chemical (Class B) fire extinguisher nearby.



  8. Remove the 2 intake manifold bracket bolts (1).
  9. Remove the 2 intake manifold bracket nuts (2).
  10. Remove the intake manifold bracket (3).


  11. Remove the 2 fuel injection fuel rail bolts (1).

    12. Note: Be careful while removing. Do not damage the injectors.

  12. Remove the fuel injection fuel rail (2).


  13. Remove 4 retaining clamps (3).
  14. Pull out the 4 injectors from the fuel injection fuel rail (1).

Installation Procedure


    Note: Coat the seal rings with lubricant.

  1. Install NEW injector seal rings.

    2. Note: Inspect the installation position and ensure correct fitting.

  2. Install the 4 injectors to the fuel injection fuel rail (1).
  3. Install 4 retaining clamps (3).


    4. Note: Be careful while Installing. Do not damage the injectors and injector seal rings.

  4. Install the fuel injection fuel rail (2).

    5. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  5. Install the 2 fuel injection fuel rail bolts (1) and tighten to  25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  6. Remove the CH-807 plugs .
  7. Connect the quick-release fitting of the fuel feed pipe.
  8. Connect the wiring harness to the fuel injectors.


  9. Install the intake manifold bracket (3).
  10. Install the 2 intake manifold bracket nuts (2) and tighten to  25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  11. Install the 2 intake manifold bracket bolts (1) and tighten to  25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  12. Connect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  13. Install the engine sight shield. Refer to Engine Sight Shield Replacement .
  14. Close the hood.
