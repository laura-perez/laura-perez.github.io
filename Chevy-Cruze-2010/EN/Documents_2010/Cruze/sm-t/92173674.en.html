Engine Coolant Thermostat Housing Installation

  1. Clean sealing surface.
  2. Install a NEW engine coolant thermostat housing seal.


  3. Install the engine coolant thermostat housing (2).

    4. Note: Screw in the 4 bolts until the engine coolant thermostat housing is in contact with the cylinder head.

  4. Install the 4 engine coolant thermostat housing bolts (3).

    5. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

    Note: It is essential to comply with the specified tightening sequence 1-4 - risk of fracture.



  5. Tighten the 4 engine coolant thermostat housing bolts to  8 N·m (71 lb in).


  6. Install the engine coolant thermostat housing retainer (1).
  7. Install the engine coolant thermostat housing retainer nut (4) and tighten to  9 N·m (80 lb in).
