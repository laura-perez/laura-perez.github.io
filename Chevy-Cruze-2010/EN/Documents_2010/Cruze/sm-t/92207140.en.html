Air Conditioning Condenser Replacement (1.6L LXV, LDE, LXT and 1.8L 2H0)

Removal Procedure
  1. Recover the refrigerant. Refer to Refrigerant Recovery and Recharging .
  2. Disconnect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  3. Remove front bumper fascia. Refer to Front Bumper Fascia Air Deflector Replacement .


  4. Remove air intake duct.
  5. Remove radiator grille support. Refer to Radiator Support Replacement .
  6. Remove A/C compressor and condenser hose from condenser. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor and Condenser Hose Replacement .


  7. Remove condenser hose bolt and condenser hose (1, 2).
  8. Remove A/C refrigerant pressure sensor. Refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Refrigerant Pressure Sensor Replacement .


  9. Remove condenser.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install condenser.
  2. Install A/C refrigerant pressure sensor. Refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Refrigerant Pressure Sensor Replacement .

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  3. Install condenser hose and condenser hose bolt and tighten to  XX N·m (XX lb in).
  4. Install A/C compressor and condenser hose from condenser. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor and Condenser Hose Replacement .
  5. Install radiator grille support. Refer to Radiator Support Replacement .


  6. Install air intake duct.
  7. Install front bumper fascia. Refer to Front Bumper Fascia Air Deflector Replacement .
  8. Connect battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  9. Evacuate and charge the refrigerant system. Refer to Refrigerant Recovery and Recharging .
