Intake Camshaft Gear Removal

Special Tools

    • EN 6347 Brace Holder
    • EN 956-1 Brace

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .



    Note: Two mechanics are required

  1. Install the EN 6347 brace  (1) and the EN956-1 brace holder  (2) in the intake camshaft gear.
  2. Loosen the locking bolts (3) from EN 6347 brace .

    3. Note: The grooves of EN 6347 brace must locate in the camshaft gear. Check for correct seating.

  3. Tighten the 2 locking bolts (3).
  4. Loosen the intake camshaft gear bolt (4) while holding the intake camshaft gear with EN 6347 brace .
  5. Remove the intake camshaft gear bolt (4).
  6. Remove the intake camshaft gear.
  7. Repeat the procedure at the intake camshaft gear.
