Fuel Tank Vent Pipe Replacement

Special Tools

    • EN 807 Closure Plugs
    • EN-45059 Angle Sensor Kit

Removal Procedure

Warning: Refer to Gasoline/Gasoline Vapors Warning in the Preface section.

Warning: Refer to Actions to Take When Working with Fuel Warning in the Preface section.

Warning: Refer to Safety Goggles and Fuel Warning in the Preface section.

  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .


  2. Remove the fuel tank vent pipe (2) from the evaporative emission canister solenoid valve (1).
  3. Close the fuel tank vent pipe (2) and the evaporative emission canister solenoid valve (1) with the EN 807 plugs .


  4. Remove the exhaust pipe front hanger bolts (2).
  5. Remove the frame rear bolt (3).
  6. Remove the frame reinforcements.


  7. Unclip the fuel tank vent pipe (1) from the 4 bracket clips (2).


  8. Unclip the fuel tank vent pipe (3) from the rear bracket clip (2).
  9. Disconnect the fuel tank vent pipe (3) from the fuel tank vent pipe connector (1).
  10. Remove the fuel tank vent pipe (3).
  11. Close the fuel tank vent pipe and the fuel tank vent pipe connector with the EN 807 plugs .

Installation Procedure
  1. Remove the EN 807 plugs .


  2. Install the fuel tank vent pipe (3).
  3. Connect the fuel tank vent pipe (3) to the fuel tank vent pipe connector (1).
  4. Clip in the fuel tank vent pipe (3) to the rear bracket clip (2).


  5. Clip in the fuel tank vent pipe (1) to the 4 bracket clips (2).


  6. Install the frame reinforcements.

    7. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  7. Install the NEW frame rear bolts (3) and tighten to  90 N·m (67 lb ft) +130° use the EN-45059 sensor kit .
  8. Install the 4  exhaust pipe front hanger bolts (2) and tighten to  20 N·m (15 lb ft).
  9. Remove the EN 807 plugs .


  10. Install the fuel tank vent pipe (3) to the evaporative emission canister purge solenoid valve (1).
  11. Clip the fuel tank vent pipe (3) into the brake, fuel feed and fuel clip (2).
  12. Connect the battery negative cable Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
